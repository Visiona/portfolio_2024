About Me

We can make it together

I'm Darek, a UX Designer with a web development background based in Dublin, Ireland. I began in front-end development and discovered a passion for user-centric design through exploring UX research. I love blending interactive design with a human-centric approach and value effective communication and collaboration.

I've designed and developed over 40 projects, focusing on simplifying complex data into intuitive, user-friendly components.

Experienced in

UX Research

User interviews, surveys, usability testing, heuristic evaluation

UI Design

Wireframes, clickable prototypes, high-fidelity mockups, responsiveness, interaction design, Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Firefly), Sketch, Balsamiq.

Front-end Development

Advanced HTML and CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Github

CMS Development

Wordpress, Ms Power Apps (Power Automate & Power BI), Webflow

Core Competencies

Agile Methodologies, Effective Communication, Storytelling, Empathy-Building, Teamwork, Project Management
