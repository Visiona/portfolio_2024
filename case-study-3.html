CONTEXT

The Accenture Senior Executive Support team, collaborating with the CEO Julie Sweet, sought to redesign a Power Point completed by Senior Managing Director candidates. When discussing the initial brief, I realised redesigning wouldn’t be an answer to their problem, that a new process would bring a real relief to their challenges. The existing process was cumbersome. Previous process consisted of gathering input from approximately 80 candidates for the Senior Managing Director role at Accenture via a pre-styled PowerPoint template. The slides submitted by applicants required extensive manual editing, resending slides to users, lot of copying and pasting to create polished presentations for final C-suite review and approval. This workflow generated overload in communication between the Support Team and Senior Executives. Since the Accenture CEO was the final reviewer, it was crucial to have the applications well-polished and easy to review.



GOALS

  • Create new submission process.
  • Reduce the extensive communication between applicants and support team so the applicants provide exactly what is expected.
  • Simplify user experience for applicants, so they can prepare forms quicker.
  • Simplify & automate process for Support Team.
  • Improve experience for leadership reviewing the candidates forms.


RESEARCH (Support Team oriented)

Before I could design the new application process, it was crucial to thoroughly understand the existing system and its pain points. To achieve this, I analyzed applicant data collected in a detailed PowerPoint presentation. This analysis revealed several issues that needed to be addressed.


Field study & user interview

I have interviewed two members of the Support Team to understand the challenges of the current project while observing the process they go through from the start to the end of preparing ppt for candidates and when these are completed, then observing the process of managing the completed slides when they extensively edit them and merge into one presentation that would normally go to CEO for review. Here is a sample of some questions I asked:

  • What do you see as the primary function of the ppt slides is?
  • What do you like about the existing process (bright sides)?
  • What don’t you like about the existing process (pain points)?
  • What improvements could be made to make this process easier or better?
  • How long do you expect the whole project to take, what are the stages?
  • Please walk me through the process form the beginning of preparing the Power Point slides, through completion up to preparing completed forms for CEO.

HIGH FIDELITY PROTOTYPE & INTERACTION

Here are some features incorporated into the prototype based on the wireframe-testing results.

Usability heuristics at every step: Error Prevention while form is not completed, it is saved, can be edited now or later; User Control though control over unwanted actions, Minimalism of read content, Consistency

High color contrast ratio of 19:6 and AAA compliance ensure excellent readability and usability for all users, including those with visual impairments. This makes it easy to navigate and quickly access information, especially for users in a rush.

Enhanced accessibility and usability by showing the text field's border highlighted on focus, during typing, and when inactive, making it clear and intuitive for users to interact with the input area.

Visibility of System Status Heuristic - tracking remaining characters in all text fields for candidates, gives more control over content size to Support Team and helps candidates draft their answers better.

RESULTS

How the project affected users and the business

The interactive design of the form interface significantly reduced the need for detailed instructions, leading to a 75% decrease in the Leadership Support Team's workload. User feedback was overwhelmingly positive, with many appreciating the form's ease of use and reliability. For example, users noted that whatever they left unfinished in the form, their data remained intact even when their connection dropped during travel. This form has now become a benchmark for similar projects at Accenture.
